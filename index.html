✨ Build Your Perfect Skincare Routine ✨

🌿 Stay-at-Home Essentials

  • Cleanser: Use a gentle, hydrating cleanser to remove impurities.
  • Toner: Balance your skin’s pH for a refreshed feel.
  • Moisturizer: Apply evenly to lock in hydration.
  • Lip Balm: Keep your lips soft and hydrated all day.
  • 💡 Elovya Tip: Even indoors, apply a light sunscreen — blue light can harm too!

☀️ Daytime Routine (Going Out)

  • Cleanser: Start fresh with a mild face wash.
  • Serum: Add Vitamin C serum for glow and sun protection.
  • Moisturizer: Use a lightweight, SPF-based moisturizer.
  • Sunscreen: Never skip — apply evenly on face and neck.
  • Lip Tint: Add a natural tint for a radiant look.
  • 💡 Elovya Tip: Always reapply sunscreen every 3–4 hours outdoors.

🌇 Evening / Party Look

  • Cleanser: Clean your face to remove daytime dust and oil.
  • Primer: Smooth base for makeup.
  • Foundation & Concealer: Blend gently for a flawless finish.
  • Eye Makeup: Define your eyes with kajal, liner & mascara.
  • Lipstick: Choose bold, confident shades by Elovya.
  • 💡 Elovya Tip: Dab highlighter lightly on cheekbones for that glow!

💖 Date Night Routine

  • Exfoliate: Gently remove dead cells for smoother skin.
  • Serum: Hydrating or brightening serum adds instant radiance.
  • Makeup Base: Use BB cream or light foundation.
  • Perfume: Spray on pulse points for long-lasting fragrance.
  • 💡 Elovya Tip: Confidence is your best glow — smile naturally!

🎉 Hanging Out with Friends

  • Toner + Moisturizer: Quick refresh for your skin.
  • Compact Powder: Reduce shine without heavy makeup.
  • Gloss: Add shimmer for a fun, effortless vibe.
  • Perfume: Keep it light and playful.
  • 💡 Elovya Tip: Go minimal — let your natural beauty shine through!

🌙 Bedtime Routine

  • Makeup Remover: Never sleep with makeup on.
  • Cleanser: Deep clean to clear pores.
  • Night Cream: Apply evenly on face & neck.
  • Eye Cream: Gently tap under eyes to reduce puffiness.
  • 💡 Elovya Tip: Sleep 7–8 hours — beauty truly starts with rest.
