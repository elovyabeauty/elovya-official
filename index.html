🌸 Build Your Perfect Skincare & Beauty Routine

🌅

Morning

Fresh & protected for the day.

  1. Cleanser: Massage on damp skin 20–30s, rinse.
  2. Toner: Pat to rebalance skin.
  3. Moisturizer: Apply while skin slightly damp.
  4. Sunscreen: Apply broadly — face, neck, ears.

💡 Elovya Tip: Wait 1–2 minutes between serum and sunscreen.

☀️

Day / Going Out

Light coverage + sun protection.

  1. Light cleanser + hydrating serum (vitamin C).
  2. Moisturizer + SPF 30–50.
  3. BB cream / light foundation and lip tint.

💡 Elovya Tip: Reapply sunscreen outdoors every 2–3 hours.

🌇

Evening / Party

Glam for the evening.

  1. Double cleanse (oil → cleanser).
  2. Primer, foundation & concealer.
  3. Eye makeup + bold lips; finish with setting spray.

💡 Elovya Tip: Use a damp sponge for an airbrushed finish.

💞

Date Night

Soft, romantic & memorable.

  1. Gentle exfoliation 1–2× weekly (if needed).
  2. Illuminating serum + light foundation & blush.
  3. Perfume on pulse points.

💡 Elovya Tip: Focus on one feature — lips or eyes.

🎉

Party

Long-wear & camera-ready.

  1. Long-wear primer + setting powder.
  2. Layer mascara or falsies for impact.
  3. Seal lipstick with liner for longevity.

💡 Elovya Tip: Blot between layers for longer wear.

🌙

Bedtime

Repair & hydrate overnight.

  1. Remove makeup completely (micellar/oil remover).
  2. Cleanse, then apply night cream & eye cream.
  3. Lip mask if desired.

💡 Elovya Tip: Sleep on a silk pillowcase to reduce friction.

💄 Cosmetic Essentials & How to Use Them

😊

Face

  • Primer — thin layer before foundation.
  • Foundation — blend with sponge or brush.
  • Concealer — dab & blend under eyes or on spots.
  • Compact/Powder — set T-zone lightly.
  • Blush & Highlighter — apples & cheekbones.
  • Setting Spray — mist to lock finish.
👀

Eyes

  • Eyeliner — draw close to lashes for definition.
  • Mascara — wiggle at roots and pull upward.
  • Eyeshadow — blend transition → lid → outer corner.
  • Brow Pencil — short strokes to mimic hair.
  • Under-eye 👁️ — apply eye cream with ring finger, pat gently.
💋

Lips

  • Lip Balm — hydrate pre-application.
  • Lip Liner — define shape to prevent feathering.
  • Lipstick — apply center→edges, blot for longevity.
  • Gloss — add shine on top.
💅

Nails

  • Nail Polish — thin layers, 2 coats for opacity.
  • Remover — gentle wipe; rehydrate cuticles after.
  • Cuticle Oil — massage daily for healthy nails.
🧴

Skincare (Core)

  • Cleanser — morning & night to remove buildup.
  • Toner — balance pH and prep skin.
  • Serum — 2–3 drops for targeted treatment.
  • Moisturizer — lock hydration after serum.
  • Sunscreen — daily application & reapply outdoors.
  • Face Mask — weekly deep care depending on type.
