🌿 Skincare Routine Style

  • 🧴 Cleanser: Apply gently in circular motions for 60 seconds, then rinse with lukewarm water.
  • 💧 Toner: Pat on the skin with a cotton pad to balance pH and prep for hydration.
  • 🧴 Serum: Use 2–3 drops, press into skin for deep absorption.
  • 🧴 Moisturizer: Massage evenly to lock in moisture and smooth texture.
  • 🌞 Sunscreen: Apply SPF 30+ 15 minutes before stepping out.
  • 🌙 Night Cream: Use as the final step before bed for skin repair.
  • 💄 Lipstick: Apply from center outward; blot lightly for a matte look.
  • 👁️ Eyeliner: Apply close to the lash line for natural definition.
  • 🧴 Foundation: Blend evenly using sponge/brush; extend towards the neck.
  • 🧴 Concealer: Dab on dark circles/blemishes; blend gently.
  • 🧴 Highlighter: Apply on high points—cheekbones, bridge of nose, and cupid’s bow.
  • 💆‍♀️ Face Mask: Use 2–3 times a week for deep hydration and glow.
  • 🧴 Exfoliator: Gently scrub once or twice weekly to remove dead skin cells.
  • 🧴 Face Mist: Spray lightly anytime to refresh and hydrate the skin.

🕒 When to Use

  • 🌅 Morning: Cleanser → Toner → Serum → Moisturizer → Sunscreen.
  • ☀️ Afternoon: Reapply sunscreen every 3 hours, use blotting paper for oil control.
  • 🌇 Evening: Cleanser → Toner → Serum → Light moisturizer or face mist.
  • 🌙 Night: Double cleanse → Toner → Eye cream → Night Cream.
  • 💃 Date Night: Primer → Foundation → Highlighter → Lipstick → Mascara.
  • 🎉 Hangout/Party: Use BB Cream → Lip Tint → Eyeliner → Compact → Setting Spray.
  • 💼 Workday: Light moisturizer → Compact → Mascara → Lip balm for natural look.
  • 🧘 Self-care Sunday: Exfoliate → Face Mask → Serum → Night Cream.
